ABOUT TWO ROBOTS

Stunning artwork and gameplay unlike any other.

The year is 2876 on a far distant planet named Rennova. Humankind has emerged from their bunkers following the War of the Five Factions. Across the barren landscapes lie mountains of steel, fields of metal, and vast expanses of damaged robots. To help rebuild Rennova to its once great glory, humans worked alongside Salvagers. With disagreements about how to restore the planet, war broke out again between opposing tribes.

HOW TO PLAY

It is both a Battle Royale and a race to build two robots.

Two Robots is a fast-paced, strategic card game of set collection and player elimination in a far distant future. Players in the game control Salvagers with special abilities attempting to build more robots than their friends. Be the first to eliminate all players or build Two Robots, the choice is yours.

Salvagers

Learn more about the heroes and their origins.

apocalypse

APOCALYPSE's anger is not too be underestimated. One wrong move is enough to have it blow up in everyone's faces.

Backstory

A prototype XI-12 nuclear robot constructed by Aatish Rao locked away after an unintended meltdown. As Agseva prepared for war, the powers-that-were remembered the stories of the XI-12 behaving strangely centuries before. Surely, it was decommissioned, they thought. To the surprise of the Agsevan government, the robot was still operational... and unexpectedly wrathful, fueled by what it perceived as betrayal from its creator and its anger toward everyone who shunned it. The robot agreed to fight for Agseva only after it was offered a heavily reconstructed frame, one that would eliminate its previous weaknesses. Taking the new name APOCALYPSE, its wrath is not easily contained. It still plays by it's own rules most of the time. Possible sequel arc would have APOCALYPSE defecting from its deal with the Agsevan government literally out of boredom and starting its own war.

peacemaker
PEACEMAKER only hopes it can survive long enough to establish peace in the new world. Attacking isn't in its game plan.
Backstory

Constructed by a group of scientists from each faction to reunite the planet. It contains a Knowledge Orb implanted with information given by each faction. Once tensions grew, the factions removed the robot's access to their information, leaving its "mind" focused on the initial purpose of keeping peace. It is only involved in the War to protect those who flocked to it for safety. Discussed character arc is the search for the Faction Data Shards. The Peacemaker feels something missing and decides to search for knowledge.

samurai
SAMURAI is quick to strike down anything or anyone in its path. His deadly Katana allows him to double-attack his enemies.
Backstory

Created as a symbol of the Ohmni Resistance's loyalty to their creed, "True to ourselves. True to our own. Our freedoms and fates rest in our hands alone." Wielding a massive electrified katana, it would also serve as the Resistance's protection against all forces the group believed would attempt to silence their message. Though the Resistance itself was met with mixed reactions from the Lysafar residents, SAMURAI quickly became the face of the faction as it was built for combat from the start. Lysafar was in no position to refuse this decision as they had not planned a proper defense. Possible sequel arc would follow SAMURAI's thought process over the Resistance's creed, debating whether its loyalty lies with the creators or with its kind.

scadvenger
SCAVENGER was built with to be quick and adaptable. It can scrounge up parts from the Rennovan wasteland better than anyone else.
Backstory

The very first Robot, MEC-0001, constructed by SURPASS during the Industrial Revelation, and the first Sentient, a term used by Rennovan scientists. It gained Sentience by listening to the conversations of its diagnostician, and because of this, it was the only MECbot aware of its imminent destruction and the only one that tried to escape. This attempt intrigued the scientists present that day. Rather than destroying the robot, they began conducting studies on the robot, trying to figure out how it was that aware of its situation. This lasted for a few centuries as no one could ever find what they were looking for.

Where did Two Robots Come From?

As pretty hardcore board gamers, the Two Robots team all sit down about once a week for a board game night. Over the years we have played many games, from party games to hero based games like Gloomhaven. What we have noticed, and something that we desired, is a party game that is quick but also has a lot of depth in strategy. We wanted it to be set in the future, with a seriously killer storyline and graphics to boot. When we first set out to start this project, we were completely unaware of how difficult it is to come up with a game that is actually fun. Future blog posts will be detailing the creative effort, so you all can experience what it’s like to create a game! Through hours of play testing with friends, family, and even strangers (thanks Craigslist!) we think we have come up with something great. We can't wait to share this world with you, from the story to the game mechanics, to eventually seeing all of you play it.
